Led a comprehensive CRM transformation project delivering 20% cost savings and 15% faster client onboarding through Agile methodologies and stakeholder alignment.

Directed a finance sector Customer Relationship Management transformation initiative utilizing Agile methodologies to deliver significant process improvements, enhanced stakeholder alignment, and superior user experience. The project focused on replacing legacy systems with ActiveCampaign CRM to streamline operations and improve client engagement.

The organization faced significant challenges with outdated CRM systems causing inefficient client onboarding processes, poor data management, and lack of integration between finance and IT systems. Manual processes led to errors, delayed response times, and frustrated stakeholders across multiple departments.

Implemented a comprehensive Agile-driven approach including stakeholder workshops, BPMN process modeling, and detailed requirements engineering. Developed 36+ user stories with 67% UAT alignment, created wireframes to resolve defects, and established traceability matrices achieving 100% test coverage. Facilitated cross-functional collaboration between finance teams, product managers, and executives to ensure business-wide buy-in.
